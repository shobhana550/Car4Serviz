Pricing

Bidding for services requested by customers is meticulously structured to accommodate small vendors and freelancers. Our revised pricing model includes a nominal 1% charge during the bidding process. In the event of an unsuccessful bid, 0.75% of the amount is promptly returned to the service provider's wallet, ensuring a fair and transparent transaction.
