About Us

Welcome to Car4Serviz Car Servicing Platform

Our first product “Car4Serviz” serves our mission to become India’s most customer-centric vehicle servicing platform; A digital market place, where they have the freedom of selecting any service provider they like, where they can enjoy services at the price their pocket allows, where service should be a place agnostic yet creating an equal opportunity environment and convenience to both the Parties.

For the past one and a half decade the passenger car population has been steadily increasing. This is mainly because the Indian economy is now considerably liberalised for all types of industries. Its observed that the total number of vehicles on the road have increased to 80 Millions whereas the 65% of servicing is still owned by the un-organized sector. This product is our small effort to organize these small vendors who are still lagging behind in the digital race.
